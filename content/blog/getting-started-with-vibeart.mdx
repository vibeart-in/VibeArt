## Getting Started

Let's walk through the basics of creating your first artwork.

### Creating Your First Image

1. Navigate to the creation page
2. Enter a detailed description of what you want to create
3. Choose your preferred style and settings
4. Click "Generate" and wait for the magic to happen

### Writing Effective Prompts

The key to great results is writing clear, descriptive prompts. Here are some tips:

- Be specific about what you want
- Include details about style, mood, and composition
- Use descriptive adjectives
- Reference artistic styles or movements

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Tip">
  Start with simple prompts and gradually add more details as you learn what works best.
</Callout>

## Advanced Techniques

Once you're comfortable with the basics, try these advanced techniques.

### Using Negative Prompts

Negative prompts help you exclude unwanted elements from your images. For example:

```
Positive: "A serene mountain landscape at sunset"
Negative: "people, buildings, cars, text"
```

### Adjusting Generation Parameters

Fine-tune your results by adjusting:

- **Steps**: More steps = more refined results (but slower)
- **Guidance Scale**: Higher values follow your prompt more closely
- **Seed**: Use the same seed to reproduce results

### Combining Multiple Styles

You can blend different artistic styles for unique results:

```typescript
const styles = ["impressionist", "cyberpunk", "watercolor"];
const blendedStyle = styles.join(" + ");
```

## Best Practices

Follow these best practices for the best results.

### Composition Tips

- Use the rule of thirds for balanced compositions
- Consider lighting and shadows
- Think about color harmony
- Pay attention to focal points

### Iterative Refinement

Don't expect perfection on the first try. The creative process involves:

1. Generate an initial image
2. Analyze what works and what doesn't
3. Refine your prompt
4. Generate again with improvements

<Callout type="success" title="Success Story">
  Many of our users find their best work comes after 3-5 iterations of refinement.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

Having issues? Here are solutions to common problems.

### Image Quality Issues

If your images aren't looking great:

- Increase the number of steps
- Try a different model
- Adjust the guidance scale
- Use more specific prompts

### Generation Errors

If generation fails:

- Check your prompt for unsupported content
- Try simplifying your prompt
- Ensure you have sufficient credits
- Contact support if issues persist
